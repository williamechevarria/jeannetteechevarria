38.65° N — Where it began

From Virginia's finest firms to the Tampa skyline

Jeannette's love for real estate began in Virginia. After extensive work as a Realtor representing buyers and sellers for some of Virginia's finest real estate firms, she extended her career to Florida — the Sunshine State.

Thirteen years on, she serves clients across Miami, Tampa, and throughout Florida — and remains licensed in Virginia. As an agent with Paramount Home Group, she specializes in luxury homes, new construction, and investor-focused opportunities, bringing strategic marketing, skilled negotiation, and sharp market insight to every transaction.

“I don't just sell homes — I position opportunities and build long-term wealth strategies for my clients.”

A proud Marine wife and mother of two sons serving in the Air Force and the U.S. Marine Corps, Jeannette holds service, discipline, and commitment at her core — and is deeply passionate about helping military families navigate relocations, VA benefits, and homeownership with confidence and clarity.
